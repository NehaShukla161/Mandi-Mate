# Mandi Mate — Video Script

**Duration:** 3:00
**Aspect ratio:** 16:9, shoot 4K for crop flexibility
**Location:** A real tomato farm in Niphad taluka, Nashik district
**Cast:** One real farmer as Ramesh, no actor
**Language:** Marathi with subtitles; one line of English voiceover at the end

## Principle

The best Gemma 3n winners put one real person at the center and let the camera hold on their face. We do the same. No infographics, no slick animations, no stock footage. Ramesh's hands and the phone are the only two heroes.

---

## Shot list

### 0:00–0:15 — Cold open. No dialogue.

| Shot | Duration | Description |
|------|----------|-------------|
| 1 | 3s | **Close-up**, Ramesh's hands cupping a single tomato. Pre-dawn blue light. Steam on his breath. |
| 2 | 3s | **Wide**, Ramesh walking between tomato rows, crate on hip. Crickets. |
| 3 | 3s | **Medium**, he sets the crate down, pulls a phone from his pocket. |
| 4 | 3s | **Tight insert**, the phone screen. Top-right: "0 bars." The OFFLINE pill pulses. |
| 5 | 3s | **Close on his face**. He's not confused. He's focused. |

No audio except ambient. No music yet.

### 0:15–0:45 — The problem, told in his own words.

Ramesh, to camera, Marathi, subtitled:

> *गेल्या महिन्यात मी अठरा रुपयांना टोमॅटो विकले. दोन दिवसांनी भाव एकतीसला गेला. दलालाने फक्त खांदे उडवले.*
>
> "Last month I sold tomatoes for ₹18. Two days later the rate was ₹31. The middleman just shrugged."

Cut to:

> *माझ्या मुलाच्या एक महिन्याच्या शाळेच्या फीपेक्षा जास्त पैसे गमावले.*
>
> "I lost more than my son's school fees for the month."

Hold on his face for 2 extra seconds after he stops speaking. This is the emotional anchor.

### 0:45–2:00 — The demo. Everything is real.

| Shot | Duration | Description |
|------|----------|-------------|
| 6 | 4s | Ramesh raises phone over the crate. Viewfinder visible. Shutter sound. |
| 7 | 3s | **Screen record, inset**: "Analyzing on device... Gemma 4 · Vision" |
| 8 | 4s | Grade card appears. Big **A**. 87% confidence. 18.5 kg. Hold on his face — tiny nod. |
| 9 | 5s | He holds the mic and speaks: *"आज विकू का?"* Phone vibrates once. |
| 10 | 6s | **Screen record**: the tool-trace screen, calls scrolling — `get_mandi_prices`, `forecast_price` × 2, `calculate_net_profit` × 3. Voiceover (Marathi farmer's daughter, in English): "His phone just ran four function calls against a two-year price forecast. Nothing left the device." |
| 11 | 3s | Recommendation card streaming in. Big Devanagari text: *थांबा दोन दिवस — पुण्याला न्या.* |
| 12 | 4s | **Audio hero beat**: he taps "Play in Marathi." The Marathi TTS plays. He listens. The camera watches him listen. |
| 13 | 4s | He nods. Slight smile. Puts the phone in his pocket. |

If you get only one magic take in the whole day, it's shot 12. Prioritize it.

### 2:00–2:30 — Outcome, real numbers.

Two days later. Back at the farm. Ramesh loading crates onto a tempo bound for Pune. Text overlay, tabular:

```
Grade A, 18.5 kg tomatoes

Sell to local middleman today     ₹333   (the default)
Sell at Nashik mandi today        ₹578
Mandi Mate recommendation         ₹644   (+93% vs middleman)
```

Ramesh, voiceover, Marathi subtitled:

> *हा फोन मला काय करायचं हे सांगत नाही. तो मला आकडे देतो. निर्णय माझा आहे.*
>
> "This phone doesn't tell me what to do. It gives me numbers. The decision is mine."

### 2:30–3:00 — The tech and the scale. Short.

Fast cuts, still on the farm. Simple white text on black between shots:

- **Offline**
- **Marathi-first**
- **Runs on ₹8,000 phones**
- **Grounded in Agmarknet data**
- **Powered by Gemma 4 — on-device, 3 GB RAM**

Final card, held for 6 seconds:

> **14 crore smallholder farmers in India.**
> **Every one of them makes this decision, every week.**
>
> **Mandi Mate.**

Fade to black. Silence.

## Production notes

**Crew.** One camera, one lavalier mic on Ramesh, one shotgun for ambient, one screen-recorded Android. Solo or two-person crew is enough.

**Light.** Shoot 05:30–06:30 and 17:30–18:30. The cold open must be the magic hour. Bring a bounce card.

**Phone screen capture.** Use Android's built-in screen recorder at 60 fps, capture separately from the camera and sync in post. Phone visible in-frame during Ramesh's interaction, inset on-screen during the tool-trace sequence.

**Audio.** The Marathi TTS playback at shot 12 must be captured clean from the phone's speaker, not re-recorded. This is the most-replicated beat — judges watch it twice.

**Ethics disclosure.** Pay Ramesh fairly. Show him the final cut before submission. The video's authenticity relies on his trust.

**Subtitles.** Burned-in subtitles in English. Font matches the app (Fraunces for emphasis, Hind for body). White with a 70% black drop shadow.

**Music.** None. Single-string plucked tanpura for 2:30–3:00 only. Let the silence do the work elsewhere.

---

## Backup plan if you can't shoot a real farmer

If the farmer shoot falls through — shoot the demo with a willing friend as "Ramesh" on any farm-looking location (a community garden works). The emotional loss is substantial but the flow still demos. Flag it honestly in the writeup: "Ramesh is dramatized; the app and data are real." Judges would rather see honest limitations than a faked documentary.
